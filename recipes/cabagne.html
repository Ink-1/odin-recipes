Cabagne Slopp

Description

A red cabagne dish with lots of nutrients and healing powers. This recipe has been passed down for thousands of years. Primitive slow cooking methods released the toxic chemicals found within the cabagne and nutralized them, making the cabagne edible for tribes accross the globe. Some say the cabagne would heal warriors coming home from battle. Broken limbs, jarred skin, or even pulminary infections were known to heal quickly after consuming this dish. Traders on the eastern sea were known to sell the cabagne at a very high price, and it was well known to pirates for obvious reasons. The red village made the majority of its profits from exporting this good accross the globe.

Ingredients

  • Rare red cabagne; sourced from the red village of India.
  • Ground corrier seeds for flavor.
  • Malt vinegar from the eastern sea.
